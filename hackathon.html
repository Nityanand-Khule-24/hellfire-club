ABOUT THE EVENT

Upside-Down Codex is a 24-hour hackathon where students and tech enthusiasts from AIML, Data Science, and Computer Science come together to innovate, collaborate, and solve real-world problems. The event encourages participants to combine creativity, technical skill, and teamwork under a time-bound challenge.
