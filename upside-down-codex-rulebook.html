General Rules

Rule · 01

Team Size: Exactly 4 members mandatory. No more, no less.

Rule · 02

Rule · 03

Duration: 24 hours non-stop hackathon. Stay vigilant, stay in the zone.

Rule · 04

Registration: Team is not registered without full payment.

Rule · 05

Problem Statement: Given ON THE SPOT at start. No prior info shared.

Any programming language, framework, or tool allowed per rules. Choose your weapons wisely.

Bring your own laptop, charger, power bank, and required gadgets.

Rule · 10

College ID card mandatory for identity verification at entry.
